// How It Works

The Process.

01
Submit Your Idea

Send us a sketch, STL file, or just a description. We'll talk through what you need and build a plan.

02
Design & Slice

We optimize your model for printing — wall thickness, supports, layer height, infill — dialing in every parameter.

03
Print & Monitor

Your part goes on the machine. We monitor remotely via OctoPrint and catch any issues before they become problems.

04
Finish & Deliver

Post-processing, quality check, and delivery. Local pickup in Fort Smith or shipped to you.
